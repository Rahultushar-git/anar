What is ANAR?

ANAR is a secure and anonymous chat platform designed for individuals who value privacy. It allows you to communicate freely without revealing your identity.

Every message sent through ANAR is temporary and automatically cleared after the session, giving users complete control over their privacy. There is no registration, no email, and no personal information required.

Whether you're sharing ideas, discussing sensitive topics, or just socializing, ANAR ensures that your identity stays hidden while your conversations remain smooth and confidential.

How It Works

  1. Open the app or web version directly in your browser.
  2. Choose a unique alias to start chatting anonymously.

  4. All chats are automatically cleared when you refresh, ensuring confidentiality.
  5. Enjoy safe, unrestricted conversations without any tracking or data collection.
