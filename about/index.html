A New Anonymous Realm

ANAR is a privacy-focused anonymous group chat platform. No login, no usernames, and no stored messages. Chat freely and securely with people globally while keeping your identity hidden. Stay private, safe, and free to communicate openly.

What is ANAR?

ANAR is a privacy-first anonymous group chat platform designed for individuals who want to communicate without revealing their identity.

All messages in ANAR are temporary and cleared after the session. No registration, emails, or personal data are required.

Share ideas, discuss topics, or socialize while keeping your identity anonymous and your conversations private.

How It Works

  1. Open the app or web version directly in your browser.
  2. Choose a unique alias to join the group chat anonymously.
  3. Send and receive messages with other participants in real-time.
  4. All chats are automatically cleared when the session ends or the page is refreshed.
  5. Connect instantly with users globally without sharing personal information.

Features

  • 100% Anonymous: No personal data collected and no tracking.
  • Temporary Messages: All chats disappear automatically after the session.
  • Minimalistic Design: Clean and intuitive interface focused on chatting.
  • Real-Time Group Chat: Chat with multiple people instantly.
  • Cross-Platform: Works seamlessly on desktop, mobile, and tablet.
  • Instant Alias: Choose a new alias anytime to refresh your identity.
